🔴

HVAC / Ventilation — Still the Most Urgent Item

Sealing the container without active climate control will defeat your humidity and variable-control goals entirely. A mini-split (12,000–18,000 BTU) and a steam exhaust vent above the espresso machine need to be in the build plan before the interior walls are finished. This is the one thing that cannot be retrofitted cheaply.

🟠

G&W Case — Confirm Refrigerated vs. Ambient Before Ordering Pastries

If the case is ambient, plan for shelf-stable items. If refrigerated, factor it into the electrical load. Either works — just know before you commit to a pastry program.
